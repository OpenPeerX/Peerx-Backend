# PeerX Code of Conduct

## Our pledge
We as members, contributors, and leaders of **PeerX** pledge to make
participation in our community a welcoming and inclusive experience for
everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, sex
characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience,
education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance,
race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our standards
Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include:
- Demonstrating empathy and kindness toward other people
- Being respectful of differing opinions, viewpoints, and experiences
- Giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback
- Focusing on what is best for the community

## Enforcement
Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be
reported to the PeerX team at **conduct@peerx.com**. All complaints will
be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.
